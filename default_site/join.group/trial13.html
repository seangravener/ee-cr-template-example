What is Club Ruby?

At its core, Club Ruby Designers is a craft community for everyone who shares a passion for paper crafts. Creating your own design profile, uploading photos of your creations and photos albums is part of the Club Ruby Basic membership and doesn't cost anything. Premium members, however, get added benefits including fun and educational videos, on-line DVDs, detailed tutorials, live classroom broadcasts and more.

What happens after my trial has ended?

Once your trial has ended, your Premium membership will simply become a basic membership. You will not loose anything in your account; everything that you've uploaded or written in your journal will still be there and accessible from your account.

Building your design profile, upload your creations,  will always be available for everyone as part of the basic membership at no cost.

You can become a Premium member at any time within your account.
