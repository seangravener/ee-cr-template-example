How do I get Club Ruby Kits?

Club Ruby kits are available to everyone on a first come, first serve basis. Advantage Members, however, are first in line each month to purchase their kits, have access to over 50 projects a month from the Club Ruby Design Team and receive many other benefits that are only included with the Club Ruby Advantage Membership.

How much do Club Ruby Kits Cost?

Club Ruby kits are available at a cost of $32.00 ( retail value 44.00) each month as a Club Ruby Designer Advantage Member.
