About Our Contests

From beginners to the most experienced paper crafters, we all need a place to find a little inspiration. That's what our Club Ruby Challenges are all about.

From Sketch Challenges to Color Combo Challenges and everything in between, we work hard to come up with fun and new ideas to get your creative juices flowing!

The Club Ruby Design Team posts two Challenges per month and all members are invited to play along. Simply make a project using the current Challenge and the Design Team samples as inspiration and upload it to your Profile. That's it! Plus every member that participates in the Challenge is entered in a drawing to win a scrap-tastic goodie bag!
